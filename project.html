Featured Research Project

Climate & Ecosystem Resilience Assessment вҖ” Jammu & Kashmir

Project Overview

This study evaluates ecosystem resilience across Jammu & Kashmir using GIS and remote sensing. It integrates climate, vegetation, and terrain variables to derive ACI, CSI, and ESI, which are combined into a final Resilience Index (RI).
